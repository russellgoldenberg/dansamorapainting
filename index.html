About us

Led by Dan Samora, Samora Painting has been painting houses in Austin for over 20 years. We work closely with interior designers and throughout the Austin area:

  • West Lake
  • Tarry Town
  • Barton Springs
  • Central Austin
  • And all surrounding areas

Our experienced crew will get you the best results, at a great price to boot. After years in the business, we have mastered and now offer other services including drywall installation and repair, and deck and cabinet staining.

We use use eco-friendly products upon request.
